IELTS Preparation

The International English Language Testing System (IELTS) examination is frequently required if you plan to emigrate, study or work overseas. This intensive study is suitable for both the IELTS General Training or Academic examination. Our Intensive IELTS preparation course helps you quickly develop skills and strategies needed to improve your IELTS band score. The course is designed for learners with limited time and who plan to take the exam in the immediate future.
In IELTS exam, four-language skills are tested i.e. reading, listening, speaking and writing. We will provide you with a very comprehensive training focused on techniques and strategies on how to master the test components.

TOEFL Preparation

Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL) is a standardized test to measures the ability of non-native English speakers to enroll in English-speaking universities. It accurately measures how well students can read, listen, speak and write in English in the college or university classroom. TOEFL is one of the two major English-language tests in the world, the other being the IELTS.
In the TOEFL exam, four-language skills are tested i.e. reading, listening, speaking and writing. TOEFL exists in two versions; internet based (iBT) and paper based. We will provide you with a very comprehensive training focused on techniques and strategies on how to master the test components.Practice, devotion and knowledge play a vital role in ensuring you score well in TOEFL. Know the different ways in which TOEFL preparation course can help you enhance your knowledge over the English language. It not just makes you proficient in English but also makes you an eligible candidate to study in some of the best universities abroad. Eloquence offer preparation for TOEFL courses cover the major areas in detail so as to make sure that you acquire a good grasp of the English language by solving the previous papers to acquire a good score in the exam. The educational courses that offer preparatory courses of this type also offer training for Cambridge exams, IELTS and TOEFL.
